About webERP

webERP is a complete web based accounting and business management system that requires only a web-browser to use.

Follow us on Bluesky social media.

The weberp.org web site is also open source. You can fork the website project on GitHub and send a pull request with additions, improvements and fixes (please post your ideas in Discussions first).

webERP has been in active development and use for more than 20 years. The primary developers are qualified accountants, Phil Daintree and Tim Schofield, whose guiding principal is to make webERP easy for business people to modify.

A wide range of features make webERP suitable for many businesses, particularly distributed wholesale, distribution and manufacturing businesses. webERP is also used in many hospitals around the world.

The webERP project also maintains webSHOP, an add-on eCommerce portal, and CounterLogicPOS, an add-on Point Of Sale system. Together, they elevate webERP to become the hub of a dispersed multi-branch retail management system.

The current release of webERP is v4.15.2 for PHP v7. webERP v5 will be released soon and include new features and support for PHP v8.
